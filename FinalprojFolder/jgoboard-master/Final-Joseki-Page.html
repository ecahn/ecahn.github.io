What are Josekis?

In Chinese, Joseki means "settled pattern." Josekis are sequences of play that result in a fair outcome for both players. If a sequence is not a Joseki, that is likely because on person made an error.

For beginner Go players, Josekis are important to learn because they teach important fundamentals.

At the professional level, players have memorized thousands of Josekis and choose which the proper ones to use based on their in-game judgement.
